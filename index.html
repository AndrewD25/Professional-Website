About Me

I am an aspiring software engineer with a love of coding, interest in graphic design, and a passion for learning. I was born and raised in Arizona, where I've lived for 17 years. Currently a senior in high school, I am also enrolled in EVIT's Software and App Design program. Here, I'm diving deep into the realm of programming, honing my skills and harnessing my creativity. I enjoy learning about all facets of the industry, but my current focus is web development.


Outside of work, I am a dedicated collector of Lego, action figures, and comic books. I love to read many types of books, but science fiction is my favorite. When I'm not reading, I enjoy playing (and sometimes creating) games on my PC. My hobbies have helped motivate some of my personal projects, such as the book inventory site I am currently building, Read Rover.
